Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from other kiosks?

Traditional kiosks (McDonald's, airports) are transactional: order food, check-in, done. EngageOS is engagement-first. We turn wait times into revenue via games, upsells, data capture, and social sharing. Average ROI is 2,000% vs 300-500% for traditional kiosks.

What if customers don't use the kiosk?

Pilot data shows 60-80% engagement rates (% of waiting customers who interact). We provide signage, staff training, and incentives (e.g., "Play trivia, win free chips"). Customers naturally engage when bored.

How long does setup take?

14-30 days depending on complexity. Week 1: Kickoff & content customization. Week 2: POS/EMR integration. Week 3: Installation & staff training. Week 4: Go-live. We handle everything.

Do you integrate with my POS/EMR system?

Yes. We have pre-built connectors for Toast, Square, Clover, Nextech, Aesthetics Pro, Zenoti, and 15+ more. Custom integrations available for legacy systems.

Is this HIPAA compliant for healthcare/med spas?

Yes. SOC 2 Type II certified. BAA signed with all healthcare clients. No PHI stored on kiosk (API lookups only). Before/after photos require digital consent + AI face blur option.

What about ongoing content updates?

Included. Quarterly updates (new trivia questions, seasonal games). Enterprise tier gets 1 custom game per year. AI-powered quiz generation coming Q3 2025.

Can I try before committing?

Yes. We offer 6-month pilot programs at 50% discount with free hardware. Fully refundable if you don't hit 500%+ ROI by Month 6.

What if I have multiple locations?

Volume discounts: 5-10 locations (10% off), 11-25 locations (15% off), 26+ locations (20% off + white-label option). Multi-location achievement system included (customers unlock badges by visiting different locations).

How do I access the admin panel as a business owner?

Business owners can access the admin dashboard at: https://chi-pins.vercel.app/?admin=true

You'll need your 4-digit PIN code (provided during setup). The admin panel includes:

  • Revenue analytics and ROI tracking
  • Menu/content management
  • POS and payment integrations
  • Customer data and engagement metrics
